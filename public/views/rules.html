How To Play

Starting out

  • All players roll 2 dice to decide play order
  • Play order determines Player #
  • All players start with 3 coins

Each turn

  • Roll 2 dice
  • After all players turns, go to mini game page

Candy

  • Candy must be used at beginning of turn
  • Purchase candy at end of turn
  • Cannot use landing coins to purchase candy

Mini Games

  • Select a mini game at the end of each round
  • Winner(s) receives
  • In the event of a battle mini game: players ante to play, and play for the pot

End of game

  • Possible Bonus Stars:
  • Most Mini-Game wins
  • Most green spaces
  • Most red spaces
  • Most candy purchased
